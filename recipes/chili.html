Easy Spicy Chili

Description

Hot and spicy chilli, with fresh vegatables included green bell peppers, red bell peppers, jalapeno, and poblano.

Ingredients

  • 2 green bell peppers
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 jalapeno
  • 1 poblano
  • 1 red or yellow onion
  • 16oz hamburger meat
  • 32oz of canned diced tomatoes
  • 2 16 oz cans of kidney beans
  • 1 16oz can of black beans or pinto beans
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder

    • optional

    • sour cream
    • shredded cheese
    • cilantro

Steps

  1. Dice all vegatables into medium sized pieces
  2. Sear hamburger meat on medium high heat until brown, add vegatables and "sweat" or cook until slightly tender.
  3. Add 32oz of canned diced tomatoes, 2 cans of kidney beans, and 1 can of black beans or pinto beans
  4. Add two cups of chicken broth, along with salt, pepper, garlic and onion powder to taste, stir and cover. Lower heat to medium and let simmer for 45min
  5. Serve in bowl and garnish with optional Ingredients
