Steak & Gravy

Description

Steak laid over a bed of rice, smothered in delicious southern style gravy!

Ingredients

  • 1lb steak cubed
  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 10.5oz can of cream of mushroom
  • 1 10.5oz can of cream of chicken
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Sear the meat on medium-high heat. add a generous amount of salt,pepper,garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Once meat has browned, add in 2 cans of cream of mushroom, 1 can of cream of chicken, and 2 cups of beef or chicken broth
  3. lower heat to medium and stir occassionally until thickend to a gravy like consistancy
  4. bring two cups of water to a boil and add 1 cup of rice, reduce heat and cover until rice has absorved all water
  5. serve by placing rice in a bowl and covering with steak and gravy.
